02/02/2023Presenting our new FOG report!

We have just released our new FOG (Fats, Oils and Grease) tracking and FOG report for those of our providers that service grease traps. If your company does not service grease traps then this new feature will not apply to you.

However, if you do service grease traps you can activate our FOG features by going to your Settings and turning on "Enable FOG Report". This will then activate the FOG report as well as a new FOG pop up screen when you service grease traps. When you do, you or your drivers will see an option to enter the total fat, oil and grease in the trap.

Please contact CRO Support with any questions involving our new FOG report update at 1-844-276-2467.

11/14/2022Customer Portal updates.

For those of our providers that have already been using it, it's not news that CRO offers a Customer Portal. For all of our providers we've updated our Customer Portal to allow your customers to request a services, swap or pickup of their deployed assets. Your customers can now also pay any outstanding invoices that have been posted in CRO Billing with Card Connect integration!

New Customer Portal requests also will now show up at the top right, next to the bell icon, from any page of CRO so you don't miss the requests. The first phase of our Customer Portal update will include a sign up process for your customers (for added security) and upcoming phases will offer additional features, so keep an eye on this space for future updates!
