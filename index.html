08/10/2024Route Finder Enhancement

What's Changed

You can now use the Route Finder on new dropoff or delivery jobs to find the perfect route for your new asset.

Here's a quick overview of how to add a stop to a route using a drop-off request

  • Create a new drop-off request and select the Recur options
  • Select Route Options and Click the new "Use Route Finder" button.
  • Search for Routes with Stops Nearby, select a Nearby Stop and Choose Add to Route
  • Complete the Dropoff Request and open the Route Stop Report

  • Open the Unoptimized Stops Section and Click the Route Name to open the Route
  • Optimize the Route with New Stop Added

For an in-depth walk-through, check out this video

Why did we make this change?

Many providers expressed the need to tell their customers what route an asset will be placed on when taking a drop-off request order.


08/10/2024Unbilled Jobs Tab

What's Changed

Certain conditions can make jobs unbillable, and an invoice cannot be generated. We have added an enhancement that shows the reasons for jobs being unbillable and a new "Unbilled" tab on the "Invoices Page" where you can collect these jobs and work toward making them billable, if necessary.

  • New Unbilled Tab
  • Jobs that cannot generate an invoice will allow you to move to the Unbilled tab or leave them where they are.
  • Merging invoices will show unbillable jobs
  • Unbillable jobs can be moved to the new Unbilled tab

For more information, check out this video

Why did we make this change?

You'll now have more visibility into which jobs are excluded from invoicing and why, ensuring easier ways to fix discrepancies and keep track of your billing.

05/01/2024Design update to CRO.

We have pushed out a small update that refines some design elements of CRO. Things should look a lot cleaner, now!

There is a chance that some of our providers will need to do an Empty Cache and Hard Reload in order for the changes to take full effect. If you notice anything amiss in the design of the software, you will most likely need to perform this quick procedure.

To do the Empty Cache and Hard Reload, simply right click anywhere within CRO and then click "Inspect" on the menu that pops up. After that, RIGHT click on the refresh button of your browser and choose "Empty Cache and Hard Reload". After you click that you can then close the window that opened on the right side of your screen.

This will FULLY refresh CRO and make sure that all updates are fully implemented. Your CRO should be better than ever now!
